My Story

Welcome! My name is Madeleine Lasky, and I am currently a student at the University of Pittsburgh majoring in Computer Science. Growing up with the skyline of towering skyscrapers and golden bridges in the distance, I have always been fascinated by the relationship between creative mindsets and innovation. My journey into the world of technology has been a rather untraditional one. Prior to computer science, I studied design and marketing at the Savannah College of Art and Design. It was during my time at SCAD, in between organizing marketing campaigns and drafting fashion lookbooks, that I realized my true passion involved the connection between art and science as opposed to a focus on solely one of those endeavors.

When I discovered Computer Science, I quickly realized the necessity of a creative mindset when engineering a solution. Although my past is untraditional, I am a firm believer in the idea that our past experiences mold us into well-rounded individuals. While some would view the prospect of a blank file as a nightmare, I approach it as a blank canvas full of potential. Whereas some may view learning a new programming language as daunting, I look at it as another medium to use in order to accomplish my vision. Although I am no longer an artist in the traditional sense, I am still a creative person at heart who aims to break the boundaries of what I believe I am capable of.
